About PLAYPOP

We started PLAYPOP because we were tired of plastic junk. Every toy in our store is hand-selected for quality, safety, and actual play value — no batteries required (well, mostly).

We partner with 50+ independent toy makers, prioritize sustainable materials, and test every single product on our own kids. If they lose interest in 5 minutes, it doesn't make the cut.
