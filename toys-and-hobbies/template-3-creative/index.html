About Roll & Roast

We opened Roll & Roast because we wanted a place where people actually talk to each other — not a bar where everyone stares at screens. Our theory: give people coffee, good food, and 800 board games, and magic happens.

Four years later, we've hosted 500+ game nights, 200+ tournaments, and countless first dates (3 of which ended in marriages — that we know of). We're more than a café — we're a community.
