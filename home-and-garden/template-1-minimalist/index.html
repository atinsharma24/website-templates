Our Services

About HAUS

Design with Intent

HAUS was founded in 2016 by Alina Voss and Erik Sørensen — two architects who believed that residential interiors deserved the same rigor and creativity as the buildings that house them.

Based in Portland with projects spanning North America and Europe, our studio of 9 designers, 2 project managers, and a dedicated procurement specialist brings a multidisciplinary eye to every project. We've completed 140+ spaces across residential, commercial, and hospitality sectors.

Our design language is rooted in Scandinavian restraint but warmed by organic textures — natural woods, handmade ceramics, woven textiles, and artisan lighting. We design for the long term, choosing materials and forms that age gracefully and resist the churn of trends.
