Our Story

Born From the Earth

Terra & Thread began in a small workshop in Portugal, where founder Ana Ferreira watched the fast fashion industry devastate local textile communities. She envisioned a different path — one where garments are created slowly, with intention, using only materials that honor the land they come from.

Today, every piece in our collection is crafted from certified organic cotton, TENCEL™ lyocell, or reclaimed deadstock fabrics. We work exclusively with Fair Trade certified workshops across Portugal, India, and Peru, ensuring living wages and safe working conditions for every hand that touches your clothes.

We believe transparency isn't a marketing tactic — it's a responsibility. That's why every product page tells you exactly where your garment was made, what it's made from, and the true cost of production.

What We're Made Of

Our Materials

We obsess over sourcing so you don't have to. Every fabric is chosen for its minimal environmental footprint and maximum comfort.

Organic Cotton

GOTS-certified organic cotton grown without pesticides or synthetic fertilizers. Uses 91% less water than conventional cotton farming.

TENCEL™ Lyocell

Made from sustainably harvested wood pulp in a closed-loop process that recovers 99.7% of solvents. Silky, breathable, fully biodegradable.

Recycled Wool

Post-industrial and post-consumer wool fibers given a second life. Diverts textile waste from landfills while delivering the warmth of virgin wool.

European Linen

Flax grown in Belgium and France with zero irrigation and zero GMOs. Natural temperature regulation and only gets softer with every wash.
