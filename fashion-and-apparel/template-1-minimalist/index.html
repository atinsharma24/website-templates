Our Story

Where Timeless
Meets Modern

Founded in 2018, Maison Élise was born from a simple belief: that every woman deserves a wardrobe that feels as effortless as it looks. We design for the woman who moves through the world with quiet confidence — who sees fashion not as a statement, but as an extension of self.

Each collection is thoughtfully developed in our Paris atelier, where we source the finest European linens, Japanese silks, and Italian cashmere. We believe in slow fashion — creating fewer, better pieces that stand the test of time and grow more beautiful with every wear.

Our artisans bring over three decades of couture experience to every stitch, ensuring that each garment meets the highest standards of craftsmanship while remaining accessible for everyday life.
