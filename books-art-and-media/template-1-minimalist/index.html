Our Story

Chapter started as a single shelf in a coffee shop. Thirteen years and four expansions later, we're home to 12,000 titles across two floors — plus Portland's coziest reading nook (the green velvet chair by the window, obviously).

We believe bookstores are civic infrastructure. A place to think, argue, wonder, and belong. Every purchase here supports a local, independent business and keeps the shelves weird.
