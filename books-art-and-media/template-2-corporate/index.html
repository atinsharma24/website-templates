About

FORMA is a contemporary art gallery in Portland's Pearl District, representing emerging and mid-career artists working across painting, sculpture, photography, and new media.

Founded by curator Diana Park, the gallery presents 8–10 exhibitions per year alongside artist talks, studio visits, and an annual emerging artist showcase. We work with private collectors, institutions, and first-time buyers alike.
