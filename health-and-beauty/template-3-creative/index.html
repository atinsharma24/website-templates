Our Philosophy

Rooted in Nature

Bloom & Root was born in a small kitchen in Vermont, where herbalist and naturopath Leah Hartwell began crafting tinctures and teas for her local community. What started as a passion project has grown into a movement — one that honors the ancient wisdom of plant medicine while embracing modern nutritional science.

We believe wellness shouldn't come in a lab coat. It should come from the earth — from the adaptogenic mushrooms of the Pacific Northwest, the turmeric fields of Kerala, and the matcha estates of Uji. Every ingredient in our products is sourced directly from small organic farms, ensuring purity, potency, and fair compensation for growers.

Our philosophy is simple: what you put in your body matters. That's why we never use synthetic fillers, artificial flavors, or unnecessary additives. Just plants, doing what plants do best.
