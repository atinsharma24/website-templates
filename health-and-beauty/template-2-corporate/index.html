About Us

Built by Dermatologists

DERMATICA was founded in 2020 by board-certified dermatologist Dr. Elena Park, who spent 15 years watching patients waste money on overpriced products filled with inactive fillers. She set out to create a line where every ingredient earns its place.

Today, our team of 12 scientists, 3 dermatologists, and 2 cosmetic chemists develops each formula in our Portland, Oregon lab. We test obsessively, publish our trial data openly, and reformulate annually based on the latest peer-reviewed research.

We believe great skincare shouldn't require a PhD to understand — or a trust fund to afford. That's why we keep our prices fair, our ingredient lists transparent, and our science approachable.
