About Us

Chronos & Co has been Portland's premier horological destination for over 25 years. Founded by second-generation watchmaker Erik Lindberg, the boutique combines the intimacy of a family business with the authority of an authorized dealer.

Every timepiece we sell receives a complimentary inspection and a 5-year service guarantee. Our in-house workshop handles everything from routine servicing to complex restoration.
