The Brand

Aurélia was founded in 2009 by master goldsmith Claire Duval with a singular conviction: fine jewelry should be as ethical as it is beautiful.

Every stone is conflict-free and traceable. Every piece is handcrafted in our Portland atelier by a team of 12 artisans. We use recycled precious metals and responsible sourcing — because luxury and conscience are not at odds.
