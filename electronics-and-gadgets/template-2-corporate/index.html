About NEXIA

Building the Connected Home

NEXIA was founded in 2019 by a team of ex-Apple and ex-Google engineers who believed smart home technology was too fragmented, too expensive, and too complex for most families.

Our mission: build an ecosystem where every device speaks the same language — no extra hubs, no subscription fees for core features, and setup that takes minutes, not hours.

Today, over 2 million homes across 30 countries run on NEXIA. We're Matter 1.3 certified, carbon-neutral certified, and rated #1 in customer satisfaction by J.D. Power for two years running.
