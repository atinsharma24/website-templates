Our Story

Built by Adventurers, for Adventurers

BASECAMP was born in a garage in Bend, Oregon in 2015. Two friends who were tired of overpriced, underperforming outdoor gear decided to build what they wished existed — trail-tested equipment at fair prices.

Today, we've shipped over 500,000 orders to 48 states, earned a 4.9 average review across all products, and our team still tests every piece of gear we sell — on real trails, real rivers, and real mountains.
