ABOUT APEX

APEX was founded in 2018 with a simple belief: fitness should be empowering, inclusive, and uncompromising in quality. We built a 12,000 sq ft space designed around four pillars — Strength, Conditioning, Mindfulness, and Community.

With 14 certified trainers, 60+ weekly classes, and 2,400 active members, APEX has become Portland's top-rated boutique fitness studio for three years running.
