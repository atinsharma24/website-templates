The Taproom

VISIT US

Our taproom and beer garden sit at the corner of a converted 1940s warehouse in Portland's Central Eastside. Exposed brick, steel beams, and 20-foot ceilings — industrial heritage meets brewery culture.

We serve all 12 taps alongside a rotating food truck lineup, with communal picnic tables, lawn games, and live music every Friday & Saturday night.
