Our Story

From a Home Kitchen to Your Table

Flour & Flame started in 2019 when Sophie and Marco Chen — a pastry chef and a coffee roaster — quit their corporate jobs and leased a tiny 400-square-foot space on Elm Street. The idea was simple: bake the best bread in town and brew coffee worth getting out of bed for.

Within six months, the line stretched around the block every Saturday morning. Neighbors became regulars, regulars became friends, and that tiny space expanded into three locations across Portland and Seattle.

Our sourdough starter, "Eleanor," is five years old and the heart of everything we bake. Every loaf is shaped by hand, slow-fermented for 48 hours, and stone-baked in our wood-fired oven. We source our flour from Cairnspring Mills, our butter from a local creamery, and our coffee beans are roasted in-house weekly.
