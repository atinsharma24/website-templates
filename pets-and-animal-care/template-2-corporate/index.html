Our Story

WAGZ started on our kitchen table — hand-packaging organic dog treats for our rescue pup, Bruno, who had every food allergy in the book. Friends wanted bags, farmers' markets wanted us, and before we knew it, we had a brand.

Today we curate 200+ products from 40 independent brands, ship to all 50 states, and donate 2% of every order to local shelters. We still taste-test everything on Bruno (he insists).
