What We Offer

Our Services

Full-spectrum veterinary care under one roof — from puppy vaccinations to advanced surgical procedures.

💉

Wellness & Vaccines

Annual exams, core vaccinations, heartworm testing, and age-specific preventive care plans for every life stage.

🏥

Surgery

Spay/neuter, orthopedic, soft tissue, and laparoscopic surgery — all with in-house anesthesia monitoring.

🦷

Dental Care

Digital dental X-rays, cleanings, extractions, and at-home oral health guidance — dental disease affects 80% of pets by age 3.

🚨

Urgent & Emergency

Walk-in urgent care Mon–Sat. After-hours emergency triage with direct transfer to our partner ER hospital.

🔬

Diagnostics & Lab

In-house blood work with 15-minute results, digital radiography, ultrasound, and cytology.

🐍

Exotic Pets

Specialized care for rabbits, reptiles, birds, and small mammals. Dr. Chen is board-eligible in exotic animal medicine.
