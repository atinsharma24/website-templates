Our Story

About FurEver

FurEver was founded in 2017 by two friends who believed grooming and training should be joyful — for pets and their humans. We're cage-free, use only organic products, and follow Fear Free and force-free protocols.

Our team of 8 certified groomers and 3 CPDT-KA trainers have helped over 5,000 pets look and feel their best. We're proud to be Portland's highest-rated grooming salon for four years running.
