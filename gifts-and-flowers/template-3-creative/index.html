Our Story

Inkwell was founded by two printmakers who fell in love with vintage Vandercook and Heidelberg presses. We believe there's nothing digital can replicate — the deep impression of letterpress, the texture of cotton paper, the weight of a well-made card in your hand.

Today, our Portland studio houses four heritage presses, a foil-stamping station, and a small-batch paper workshop. Every piece is printed, trimmed, and packed by our team of six.
