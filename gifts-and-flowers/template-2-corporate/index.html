About GIFTED

GIFTED was born from a frustration: finding meaningful, beautifully presented gifts shouldn't be this hard. So we built a shop that does the curating for you — 150+ artisan products from 35 independent makers.

Every item is hand-selected, every box hand-wrapped with care, and every order ships with a handwritten note. We believe the way you give matters as much as what you give.
